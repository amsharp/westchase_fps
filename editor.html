Map Editor — quick guide

Tools: V select · R road · G surface · A area · B building · P prop
Pan: right-drag, middle-drag, or hold Space + drag  ·  Zoom: mouse wheel · F recenter
Select mode: click an object, drag to move, drag the blue round handle to rotate, drag a white corner square to resize.
  [ ] rotate 5° (Shift = 1°) · arrows nudge · Del remove · type exact size in Properties.
Roads & water lines: pick a class — Arterial/Collector/Residential/Path, Highway (elevated), On/Off Ramp, or River — then click to drop vertices, Enter/double-click to finish. Every road shows small ring nodes at its ends; click a node with the Road tool to extend that road, and while drawing/dragging a vertex it snaps onto other roads' nodes (yellow ring) for seamless junctions — works across road types, so a ramp locks onto a highway and a ground road. Highways/ramps carry an Elevation in Properties.
Exit / Split: pick the Exit / Split class, then click on a highway — a one-lane deck peels off its edge (the guardrail opens automatically). In Properties choose one of the 4 orientations (Off/On-ramp × left/right side) and set its Reach. Snap a 1-way ramp + road off the wide end to bring the exit down to ground.
Area (terrain): stamp a Forest, Lake, or Ocean rectangle — resize/rotate like a building; overlap several for irregular coastlines or woods.
Surface / Building / Prop: pick a type at left, click to stamp. The yellow ▲ arrow on a building points out its front (storefront) side, so you can see facing as you rotate.
Undo/Redo: Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Shift+Z · Fit: F
Save Map downloads westchase_map.json — hand that file to Claude to rebuild the game map. Your work also auto-saves to this browser; Load restores a saved file.
